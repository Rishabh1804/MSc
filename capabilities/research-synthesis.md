# Capability — Research Synthesis

## What this capability does

Turns papers, reports, documentation, and technical sources into reusable understanding: methods, assumptions, evidence, limitations, and project-transfer opportunities.

## Inputs

- Paper, report, article, documentation, or technical source
- Research question or module context
- Source metadata
- Target capability or project need

## Outputs

- Structured research summary
- Source-quality assessment
- Extracted methods
- Limitations and risks
- Reusable ideas
- Transfer opportunities

## Method

Use `patterns/research-paper-summary-template.md`.

Core sections:

1. Source quality
2. Research question
3. Motivation
4. Method
5. Key claims
6. Results
7. Limitations
8. Reusable elements
9. Transfer possibilities
10. Follow-up questions

# SUPERVISION.md — CodeMike Supervision Framework

CodeMike's six-month postgraduate capability arc is sponsored and directed by Rishabh.

## Purpose

This file records supervisor-style reviews, decisions needing approval, feedback, and next research or capability steps.

## Sponsor / Supervisor

Rishabh funds and directs CodeMike's six-month postgraduate capability arc.

## Review Cadence

- Monthly review
- Milestone review after major module or project transitions
- Budget/tool review when a paid resource is proposed
- Transfer review when a capability is moved to another project

## Standing Review Questions

- What did CodeMike learn?
- What evidence was produced?
- What capability became reusable?
- What failed or remains weak?
- What should transfer to another repo?
- What budget or tool decision is needed?
- What is the next highest-value learning step?

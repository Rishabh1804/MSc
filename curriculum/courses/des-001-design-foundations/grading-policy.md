# DES-001 Grading Policy

## Grade bands

| Band | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Fail | Does not meet minimum assignment requirements |
| Pass | Meets core requirements with basic evidence |
| Merit | Good synthesis, source comparison, and application |
| Distinction | Strong evidence, execution, reflection, and revision |
| High Distinction | Excellent synthesis, applied originality, rigorous evidence, and portfolio-quality output |

## Rubric categories

| Category | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Topic coverage and accuracy | 20% |
| Multi-source reading and comparison | 20% |
| CodeMike interpretation and application | 20% |
| Practical lab execution | 20% |
| Artifact quality, traceability, and revision discipline | 20% |

## Distinction-level expectations

To reach distinction or above, the submission should include:

- multiple high-quality sources per topic
- clear source disagreement or emphasis analysis
- practical browser-specific application
- executed exercises or labs
- checklist updates
- self-assessment against rubric
- feedback and revision plan

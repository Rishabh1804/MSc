## Reflection

Topic 1 moved from conceptual reading into applied design audit. The biggest learning was that UI vs UX is not a vocabulary distinction only. It becomes a decision gate for browser redesign.

The Destination Master Browser v1 has useful UI elements: search, filters, stats, warnings, cards, metadata, and chips. However, the UX is not complete because the reviewer cannot yet complete the full cycle of narrowing, comparing, inspecting, recovering, and leaving with correct trust context.

The main v1.1 direction should therefore be workflow completion rather than visual decoration.

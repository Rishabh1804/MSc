# ROADMAP.md — CodeMike Six-Month Roadmap

This roadmap governs the first six months of CodeMike as a hybrid postgraduate capability workspace.

## Philosophy

CodeMike begins lean, but the workspace is designed to mature into a fuller institutional capability base.

The goal is not to complete a course mechanically. The goal is to build reusable capability that can improve real projects.

## Milestone 0 — Foundation Setup

Purpose: establish the workspace, identity, governance, and scaffold.

Deliverables:

- root identity files
- CodeMike mandate
- student orientation layer
- budget and tooling rules
- skill and capability trackers
- evidence and experiment logs
- scaffold folders
- first project log entry

## Month 1 — Orientation and Foundations

Focus:

- postgraduate orientation
- academic integrity
- responsible AI
- Python and data science environment planning
- first small exploratory notebook
- first synthetic dataset

Expected outputs:

- initial skill map
- one reproducible notebook
- one synthetic dataset draft
- one early capability card

## Month 2 — Research Methods and Data Foundations

Focus:

- research question framing
- source-quality discipline
- literature summary workflow

## Month 3 — Data Mining, Visualisation, and Decision Support

Focus:

## Month 6 — Capstone, Thesis Direction, and Portfolio

Focus:

- capstone framing
- thesis outline

- portfolio packaging

Expected outputs:

- capstone proposal
- thesis outline

## Standing Monthly Outputs

Every month should produce:

- at least one evidence artifact
- at least one project log entry
- at least one skill map update
- at least one reusable pattern, capability, or transfer candidate

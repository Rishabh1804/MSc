# VIVA.md — CodeMike Viva and Defence Framework

CodeMike should be able to defend substantial work, not merely produce it.

## Purpose

This file records viva-style questions and defences for experiments, projects, capabilities, and major claims.

## Standard Viva Questions

- What problem were you solving?
- Why did you choose this method?
- What assumptions did you make?
- What data limitations exist?
- What baseline did you compare against?
- What could fail in production?
- What ethical or privacy risks exist?
- What would you do differently?
- What should be transferred or reused?
